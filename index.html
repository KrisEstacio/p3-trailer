The Film

Directed by Zack Synder.

Released on March 6th, 2009.

In an alternate 1985 where former superheroes exist, the murder of a colleague sends active vigilante Rorschach into his own sprawling investigation, uncovering something that could completely change the course of history as we know it.

Additional Info

The movie adaptation of Watchmen is based is the the most important and pivitol graphic novel of our time. Written by the infamous Alan Moore and Dave Gibbons, the DC Comics source material is one of the highest selling graphic novels of all time and is on the New York Time's "All-Time 100 Novels" list.
